How to Choose a Fisetin Extract Supplier

Fisetin extract sourcing should start with Cotinus coggygria identity, target assay level, HPLC or method language, sample availability, and a COA/TDS path that matches the grade under review.

View product page Ask about fisetin

Supplier questions buyers should ask first

A useful fisetin inquiry should identify whether the buyer is reviewing an 85% grade, 98% grade, or another project-specific target. The first response should make it clear whether the quote, sample, method language, and document path all refer to the same material.

  • What fisetin percentage is tied to the quoted grade?
  • Is the botanical source stated as Cotinus coggygria?
  • Is HPLC or other method language available for QA comparison?
  • Can COA/TDS be routed for screening, sample review, or first PO planning?
  • Does the supplier distinguish stock route from replenishment or made-to-order timing?

What to compare beyond price

Fisetin is usually reviewed as a premium specialty active, so a low unit price is not enough. Buyers should compare grade fit, assay clarity, document relevance, sample timing, MOQ, packing, and whether the supplier can answer procurement and QA questions in the same conversation.

  • Specification match before supplier ranking.
  • Sample path before production quote confidence.
  • COA/TDS relevance to the same grade under review.
  • Commercial lead time by grade and destination.
RFQ template

Send a grade-led fisetin inquiry

Hello Essence Source team, we are reviewing fisetin extract for [application]. Our target grade is [85%, 98%, or other specification], and we need to compare [method language, COA, TDS, sample timing, MOQ, or lead time]. Please advise available grade options, sample path, packing, destination assumptions, and COA/TDS availability for [first-order quantity] shipping to [destination].

Technical fit

Confirm assay level, method language, botanical identity, carrier status if relevant, and whether the product page specification matches the inquiry.

QA fit

Confirm which COA/TDS path is available for screening, sample review, or order planning, and whether any additional testing files can be discussed for qualified B2B review.

Commercial fit

Confirm sample quantity, MOQ, packing, estimated lead time, destination, and whether the request is exploratory or tied to a near-term first PO.
