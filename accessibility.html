Accessibility

Accessibility Statement

The static front end is built to support keyboard navigation, semantic HTML, alt text, visible focus states, and reduced motion preferences.

Current Measures

Buttons, menus, forms, and accordions are all accessible by keyboard. Colors and contrast are chosen for common WCAG 2.2 AA expectations.

Contact for Assistance

If you encounter an accessibility barrier, contact info@essencesourceusa.com.
