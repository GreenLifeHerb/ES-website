About

Essence Source as a U.S. B2B Botanical Ingredients Front End

The site is intentionally commercial, not retail. It exists to shorten the distance between product screening and a qualified sourcing conversation for U.S. buyers.

Who it serves

Brand managers, sourcing teams, contract manufacturers, QA reviewers, and product developers in the U.S. market.

What it promises

Clarity on what is sold, why it is credible, and how a buyer can act without searching through generic brochure content.

What it avoids

Retail pricing, consumer medical claims, and unsupported statements about product legality or approvals.
